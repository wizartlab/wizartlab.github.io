About Us

Based primarily in Chennai, SPB Impex is an integrated global trader catering to a wide customer base, offering services and products of the highest quality range at extremely competitive prices.

Our product range extends all the way from metals & minerals to financial consulting services, and everything in between. Equipped with a focus driven and a really technically sound sales and execution team, SPB Impex aims to establish itself in the global market as a client focused, trustworthy presence. Our wide range of solutions and services ensured that they can be tailored to cater to the clients’ specific requirements.

Over a very short period of time, we have managed to emerge as key commodity trading players in India, soon to mark our presence at a global scale.

Our Vision

Driven by professionalism, excellence and integrity, we aim to explore and expand our business potential to the maximum possible limit while focusing on the high quality of our associated services and products. We see ourselves establishing ourselves successfully in the Commodity Trading Industry, both locally and internationally. We wish to build relationships that are equal parts reliable and dynamic as we believe in mutual growth and symbiosis above all else.

Our business dealings are extremely transparent; as we offer a partnership rather than a mere transaction. Hence our goals are always in tandem with those of our customers, which helps us serve them in the most productive manner.
